Situation Report | ACDC alert - undiagnosed febrile illness — San Diego Region

Date: 2025-01-01

SitRep: #1 · Incident ID: SD-2025-EV

Executive Summary

2025-01-01: A 30 year old male in Southern California presented to the emergency department complaining of fever, tiredness, aches. On examination he has a temperature of 38.3C and is jaundiced. His ALP is 220U/ml with platelets of 10. Over the course of the day he becomes progressively more unwell and begins bleeding profusely.

Epidemiology

What we know

  • Initial cluster identified 10 days ago in a university setting; subsequent transmission observed in community settings.
  • Presentation: acute respiratory symptoms (fever, cough, shortness of breath). Median age: 34 (range 5–82).
  • Transmission: likely droplet and close-contact; investigations into aerosol spread ongoing.

Surveillance notes

Testing capacity has been scaled up; PCR-based assays are used for confirmation. Genomic sequencing ongoing to determine lineage and transmission chains.
